About

This website was made for a project done in Graphic Design 2 at OCAD University. The purpose of this assignment is to attempt to develop an older form of website: a resource for information. The goal of this website is to be purely informational, free of ads or clutter from the online world and to be a space for information to lay for those who are interested. Using skills learned throughout the semester in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, again, the emphasis for this website is purely to make information freely accessible as how it was intended when the World Wide Web first was made. I hope for all scrolling through my website that intention shines through and you maybe learn a thing or two!
